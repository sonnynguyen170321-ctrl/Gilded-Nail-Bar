# Gilded Nail Bar — Social Media Content Strategy

**Premium Visual Content Plan — June 2026**

*Produced for the owner. This document outlines every piece of content needed for the first 90 days of social media marketing, with detailed creative direction for photography and videography.*

---

## 01 — Content Philosophy

Every piece of content from Gilded Nail Bar should feel like a page from an editorial lookbook, not a salon's social feed. The visual language is:

| Quality | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Warm** | Golden hour tones, beige/cream dominance, bronze shadows |
| **Textural** | Every image should make you feel the material — linen, concrete, gold leaf, ceramic |
| **Still** | No frantic energy. Hands at rest. Slow movements. Quiet luxury. |
| **Precise** | Macro details, sharp focus on craft. The work speaks. |
| **Sparse** | Negative space is a feature. Let one element command the frame. |

### The Golden Rule

If you wouldn't frame it and hang it on a gallery wall, don't post it.

## 02 — Content Pillars

Four content pillars, each with a specific visual treatment:

### Pillar A — The Craft (40% of content)

Close-up documentation of nail artistry. This is the portfolio.

| Visual Style | Examples |
|-------------|----------|
| Extreme macro (2:1) | Gold foil application, brush strokes, chrome powder, gel curing |
| Before/After | Natural hand → finished manicure in same lighting |
| Process detail | Cuticle work, gel building, apex formation, file work |
| Final reveal | Slow pan across finished nails against linen, stone, or ceramic |

**Camera:** Macro lens (90mm–105mm), f/2.8–f/4, natural window light with white reflector
**Color:** Warm shift +300K, shadows pushed to bronze, highlights to cream
**Motion:** Slow-motion video at 60fps or 120fps

### Pillar B — Atmosphere (25% of content)

The salon as a physical experience. Lifestyle imagery that sells the feeling.

| Visual Style | Examples |
|-------------|----------|
| Interior details | Linen curtains diffusing light, concrete texture, brass fixtures, ceramic vessels |
| Guest experience | Hands in soak bowl, receiving massage, selecting color from swatches |
| Product styling | Polish bottles arranged on marble, towels stacked on shelf, flowers in ceramic vase |
| Natural light studies | Sunbeam across reception desk, shadow patterns through blinds at 4PM |

**Camera:** 35mm or 50mm prime, f/1.8–f/2.8 for environmental shots
**Color:** Same warm grade, slightly desaturated, film grain added
**Motion:** Static shots with gentle push-in or pull-out, 24fps

### Pillar C — Education (20% of content)

Position the salon as experts. Build trust and authority.

| Visual Style | Examples |
|-------------|----------|
| Technique breakdown | "Why we use Russian dry technique" — split screen comparison |
| Ingredient spotlight | "French Green Clay — the mineral that draws impurities" — macro of clay |
| Aftercare tips | "How to make your gel manicure last 3 weeks" — text overlay on lifestyle |
| Myth busting | "Why you shouldn't cut your cuticles" — animated graphic |

**Camera:** Mix of macro and talking-head style. Clean, well-lit, simple background.
**Color:** Standard brand grade, text overlays in IBM Plex Mono, cream background.
**Motion:** Clear, instructional. Step-by-step labeled sequences.

### Pillar D — Editorial & Culture (15% of content)

Brand-building content that positions Gilded within the broader luxury/design world.

| Visual Style | Examples |
|-------------|----------|
| Typographic quotes | Brand phrases on cream background in Cormorant Garamond italic |
| Mood boards | Collages of textures: linen, concrete, gold, ceramic, botanical |
| Local culture | McKinney Ave scene, Uptown architecture, Dallas design spots |
| Collaborations | Behind-the-scenes with photographers, stylists, local brands |

**Camera:** Varies. Typographic content is designed, not photographed.
**Color:** Strict brand palette only. No deviation.

## 03 — Content Production Briefs

### 3.1 Photoshoot Brief — "The Hands"

**Goal:** Create a library of 30–50 hero images of finished nail work for the gallery and social feed.

**Set Design:**
- 3 surfaces: raw linen (cream), honed concrete (warm gray), matte ceramic (cream)
- 1 prop per shot max: gold leaf flake, ceramic bowl, dried botanicals, brass tool
- Natural window light only (north-facing preferred)
- White foam core reflector for fill

**Shot List:**

| # | Description | Composition | Lens |
|---|-------------|-------------|------|
| 1 | Hands resting on linen, palms down, gold foil accent on ring finger | Vertical, tight crop above wrist | 105mm macro |
| 2 | Hands holding ceramic bowl, water surface visible | Horizontal, waist-level | 50mm |
| 3 | Single finger with gold leaf macro — extreme detail | Vertical, 2:1 macro | 105mm macro |
| 4 | Both hands open, palms up, resting on concrete | Horizontal, full frame | 50mm |
| 5 | Hand reaching for brass nail file on linen | Candid, motion blur on file | 35mm |
| 6 | Nail art close-up — geometric line work | Vertical, diagonal composition | 105mm macro |
| 7 | Hands folded, resting on knee, natural pose | Horizontal, environmental | 35mm |
| 8 | Polish bottle next to finished nails, same color | Still life, top-down | 50mm |
| 9 | Hand with ring, gold foil manicure, ceramic backdrop | Vertical, editorial | 85mm |
| 10 | Hands interacting with raw linen fabric | Horizontal, textured | 50mm |

**Color Grade (all images):**
```
White Balance: +350K warmth
Shadows: Tint #584638 at 15% opacity
Highlights: Tint #f8efe1 at 10%
Contrast: +15
Clarity: -10 (soft, not sharp)
Grain: +8 (film)
Black Point: Lifted to #1a1511 (no pure black)
```

### 3.3 Video / Reel Brief — "The Process"

**Goal:** 10 short-form vertical videos (15–30 seconds) for Reels and TikTok.

**Equipment:** Smartphone with gimbal (DJI OM6 or similar) or Sony a7S III
**Format:** 9:16 vertical, 4K, 60fps (slow down to 24fps timeline for smooth motion)
**Audio:** Original sound — ambient salon noise (water, ceramic clink, soft music) or recorded voiceover
**Music:** Classical piano, ambient drone, or warm lo-fi — no vocals, no trendy tracks

**Reel Concepts:**

| # | Concept | Visuals | Text Overlay |

| 3 | **Before → After** — 10s | Split screen or whip transition. Dry cuticles → finished manicure. | "Russian dry technique. No water. No shortcuts." |
| 4 | **Gel-X Extension** — 20s | Time-lapse of full Gel-X application in 20 seconds. | "From natural nail to structured extension. 90 minutes of craft." |

| 6 | **The Massage** — 15s | Hand receiving warm oil massage. Slow, intimate. | "Hands that work deserve hands that rest." |

## 07 — Content Production Checklist

### Before Each Shoot

- [ ] Clean all surfaces — lint-free linen, streak-free ceramic
- [ ] Prepare 3–5 nail looks (different colors, finishes, techniques)
- [ ] Set up lighting — natural window light only, check time of day
- [ ] White reflector positioned opposite light source
- [ ] Camera battery charged, memory card formatted
- [ ] Props staged: ceramic bowl, brass file, dried lavender, gold leaf
- [ ] Shoot both horizontal (for feed) and vertical (for Reels/Stories)
- [ ] Test shot — check white balance and exposure

### During Shoot

- [ ] Shoot in RAW (photos) or LOG (video) for color grading flexibility
- [ ] Capture each setup with 3 compositions: wide, medium, macro
- [ ] Nail polish colors should match the brand palette or be neutral tones
- [ ] Keep hands natural — no unnatural poses, no splayed fingers
- [ ] Shoot extra B-roll: product details, lighting, textures

### After Shoot

- [ ] Color grade all images to brand spec (see 3.1)
- [ ] Export web-optimized: 1080px wide for feed, 1080×1920 for Reels
- [ ] File naming: `gilded_[content-type]_[descriptor]_[date].jpg`
- [ ] Upload to cloud backup + create social media drafts folder

## 13 — Video Editing Presets

For consistent video content, apply these settings in CapCut / Premiere / LumaFusion:

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Color LUT | Warm cream lift, bronze shadows (see 3.1) |
| Grain | 8% film grain overlay |
| Text Font | IBM Plex Mono (overlays), Cormorant Garamond (titles) |
| Text Color | `#f8efe1` on dark backgrounds, `#584638` on cream |
| Lower Thirds | IBM Plex Mono, 0.65rem, all-caps, letter-spacing 0.1em |
| Music | Classical piano, ambient drone, or warm lo-fi |
| Transitions | None — hard cuts only. No wipes, slides, or effects. |
| Speed Ramp | Slow-motion segments at 40% speed for process shots |
