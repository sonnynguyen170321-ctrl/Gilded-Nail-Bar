Slides

Slide 1 — Title HOW TO MAKE YOUR GEL MANICURE LAST 3 WEEKS
Six aftercare habits from our artists.
Slide 2 — Hydration OIL DAILY
Apply cuticle oil every morning and night. Gold Serum Cuticle Oil ($28) — jojoba and argan with 24k gold flakes. Dry cuticles lift. Hydrated cuticles hold.
Slide 3 — Gloves GLOVES FOR WATER
Dishwashing, cleaning, gardening — wear gloves. Water weakens the gel bond. 10 minutes of gloves = 3 extra days of wear.
Slide 4 — No Tools NEVER PICK OR PRY
Picking removes your natural nail plate. Book a 10-minute maintenance appointment instead.
Slide 5 — Moisture HAND CREAM AFTER WATER
Every time you wash your hands, apply cream. The Organic Hand Ritual Cream ($32) is lightweight and gel-safe.
Slide 6 — Sun + Summary SUN PROTECTION + SCHEDULE
UV exposure degrades gel color — apply SPF to hands. Book your fill at 2.5–3 weeks. Don't wait for lifting.
