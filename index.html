GUIDANCE & ETIQUETTE

Frequently Asked Questions

Our structured gel manicures typically last 2–3 weeks with proper aftercare. The key is the dry e-file prep and structured overlay technique we use — this prevents lifting at the cuticle and extends wear time significantly beyond traditional wet manicures. We recommend a refresh at the 2-week mark for optimal appearance.

A Russian dry manicure uses an electric e-file to precisely remove cuticle tissue without soaking in water. This prevents swelling of the nail plate and allows for a cleaner, longer-lasting application. The result is a perfectly clean cuticle area that enhances the longevity of any polish or enhancement. It requires specialized training — our artists are certified in this method.

Arrive with clean, bare nails — free of polish, gel, or extensions. Avoid applying hand lotion or cuticle oil on the day of your appointment, as this can interfere with adhesion. For pedicures, wearing open-toed shoes or sandals is recommended. If you have reference images for your desired design, bring them along. We also suggest arriving 5 minutes early to complete any remaining paperwork and select your polish shades.

Avoid using your nails as tools (opening cans, peeling labels). Wear gloves when washing dishes or using cleaning products. Apply cuticle oil daily to maintain flexibility around the cuticle area — we recommend our Moroccan Argan Oil blend available in our artisanal shop. If a lift or snag occurs, do not pick at it — contact us for a professional repair to prevent damage to the natural nail.

We maintain an intimate, quiet salon environment and respectfully request that guests attend their appointments solo. Our lounge seating is reserved for clients receiving services. However, we welcome groups for private events — inquire about booking the full salon for bridal parties, birthdays, or corporate gatherings of 4–16 guests.
